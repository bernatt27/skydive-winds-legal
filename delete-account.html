🗑️ How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  • Open Profile
  • Scroll to the Account section
  • Tap Delete account
  • Confirm the deletion

The deletion flow is intended to be completed directly inside the application, without the need to email support.

⚠️ What happens when you delete your account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account, the data associated with it is removed, which may include:

  • Your user profile
  • Your jump history
  • Your favorite and recent dropzones
  • Your Home DZ
  • Your authentication/login account
  • Other information directly linked to your user profile within the app

📱 Data that may remain on your device

After deleting your account, some local or temporary information may remain briefly on your device until the app refreshes its state or until you remove the application.

This may include local cache, preferences, or temporary system-stored data.

If you want to make sure all local remnants are removed from your device as well, you can sign out and uninstall the app after deleting your account.

📩 If you cannot access the app

If you cannot sign in or cannot use the in-app deletion feature, you can contact us at:

skydivewindssupport@gmail.com

Please state that you want to delete your account and include the email address associated with your Skydiving Winds account.

⏱️ Processing time

When deletion is initiated from the app, the process is intended to run immediately or within a short technical timeframe. For manual support requests, we will review and process them within a reasonable period.
