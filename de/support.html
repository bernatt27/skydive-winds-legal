Support

How to contact support

Contact: support@skydivewinds.com

You can also use the in-app feedback form for technical issues, bugs, suggestions or privacy-related requests.

What support can help with

  • Sign-in, account creation, verification or password reset issues.
  • Jump log, favourites, profile or sync issues.
  • Notification problems.
  • Weather data anomalies or dropzone data corrections.
  • Privacy, export, deletion or other legal/privacy requests.
  • Technical bugs or unexpected crashes.

What to include in your message

  • The email address linked to your account, if any.
  • Device model, operating system and app version.
  • A clear description of the issue.
  • Steps to reproduce the problem.
  • Screenshots, if useful.

Please do not send passwords, one-time codes or unnecessary sensitive information.

Response times

Support is provided on a reasonable-efforts basis. There is no guaranteed response time, but messages are normally reviewed as soon as reasonably possible.

Weather and safety issues

If you believe the app displayed inaccurate weather information, include the dropzone, approximate date/time, the value that looked wrong and any comparison source you used. This helps investigate provider or interpretation issues.

Skydiving Winds is not an emergency service. Do not rely on the app or these pages for urgent operational or rescue decisions.
