Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

📋 Who we are

Skydiving Winds is a mobile application designed for skydivers, providing weather information and support tools for jump planning and post-jump review. This policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and how you can manage your information.

Contact: skydivewindssupport@gmail.com

📍 Location data

The app may request access to your location only while the app is in use and only if you allow it. Location is used solely to:

  • Load nearby wind and weather conditions
  • Show dropzones near your position
We do not store your GPS location on our servers. Coordinates are used in real time to query weather services and are discarded once the response is received.

🪂 Jump log data

If you create an account, you may voluntarily record your jump history. This data may include:

  • Jump date, time, and dropzone
  • Exit and opening altitudes
  • Jump type and equipment used
  • Personal notes and incidents
  • Weather conditions optionally saved with the jump

This data is stored in our database so that you can access and manage it across your devices. You can delete it at any time from within the app or by contacting support.

👤 Account data

If you register, we may process the following data:

  • Login email address
  • Name or alias (if provided)
  • Usage preferences: altitudes, wind limits, units, and disciplines
  • Registered equipment (optional)
  • Favorite dropzones and Home DZ

Authentication and credential management are handled through Supabase Auth. We do not access your password in plain text.

You may use parts of the app without an account. In that case, information remains on your device except for the queries required to obtain weather data from external services.

🌐 Third-party services

The app uses external services to operate:

  • Open-Meteo — weather data provider. It may receive coordinates in order to return wind and forecast data.
  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. We use this service to manage accounts and store data linked to your profile and jump log.

We apply data protection measures and choose suitable providers for processing user information. If you need more detail about providers or data location, you can contact us.

💾 Local storage on your device

To improve the user experience, the app may locally store certain preferences and temporary data, such as:

  • Measurement units and usage preferences
  • Screen cache or recent data
  • Information required to keep you signed in according to the authentication setup available on your platform

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking technologies inside the app.

✉️ Communications

We do not send marketing emails.

The only emails you may receive are those required for account operation, for example:

  • Email verification when registering
  • Password reset or password change links, if requested
  • Support replies if you contact us

🗑️ Your rights and control over your data

You can:

  • Access your data from within the app
  • Update or correct your profile information
  • Delete your account and related data from the app
  • Request assistance for export or deletion by contacting support

⚖️ Informational use and liability

Skydiving Winds is an informational support tool. The weather data displayed comes from external services and models and does not replace the judgment of the pilot, instructor, manifest, or dropzone operations staff.

The decision to jump or not to jump is always the sole responsibility of the user.
