Support

Last updated: March 2026

📩 Contact

If you need help with Skydiving Winds, you can contact us at:

skydivewindssupport@gmail.com

We aim to reply within a reasonable time, usually within a few business days.

🛠️ What we can help with

  • Login or sign-in issues
  • Email verification or password reset problems
  • Errors when saving your logbook or profile
  • Location, permission, or weather data issues
  • Dropzone data corrections
  • Privacy, export, or data deletion requests
  • Technical issues or unexpected app crashes

📋 What to include in your message

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include if possible:

  • Device model
  • Operating system and version
  • App version
  • Short description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce it
  • Screenshots, if useful
Important: please do not send passwords, access codes, or unnecessary sensitive information.

🔐 Account, privacy, and data

If your request is related to your account or personal data, you can also review:

  • Privacy Policy
  • Terms of Use
  • Delete account

If you want help with data export or deletion, please mention it clearly in the subject of your email.

🌦️ About weather data

Skydiving Winds is an informational support tool. If you notice unusual weather values or differences between app data and real conditions at a dropzone, you can contact us with:

  • The affected dropzone
  • Approximate date and time
  • Which value seemed incorrect
  • Any comparison source you may have
The information shown in the app does not replace the operational judgment of the dropzone, pilot, instructor, or safety staff.

🧾 Common requests

I am not receiving the verification or password reset email.

Please check spam, junk, or promotions folders. If it still does not arrive, contact us from the same email address you used in the app.

I want to delete my account.

You can do it from the app in the profile section. You can also find instructions at Delete account.

I want to correct dropzone data.

You can use the in-app suggestion option or contact us with the correction details.
